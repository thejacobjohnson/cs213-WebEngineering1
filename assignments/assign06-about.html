About Us

How we make dreams into realities

Our Mission

Our mission is to help individuals learn and grow academically and professionally so that they can better achieve their dreams in the world. We do this by making sure our students learn from proven industry leaders, have the latest technology at their disposal, have easy access to assistance, and are connected with professionals for quick integration into the workforce after graduation.

Proven Leaders

With industry leading professionals as your professors, each degree is tailored to not only teach you what you need to know for a test, but prepare you for your future career. Small class sizes help you to get the one on one interaction you need to succeed.

Current Technology

Few things are as frustrating as spending years of time and thousands of dollars learning and preparing for your future career, than to find out that what you learned was all out-dated. We take extra care to ensure the technology and techniques you learn and use at the University of Bulldogs is always current.

Easy to Access Advisers

Our advisers love to help! We make sure to balance our staff so that advisers are easy to schedule, and can help with drop in appointments at any time. They are specifically chosen as people who love to help others. Come drop in and meet your adviser, get help planning out your dream, and find why our advisers are rated one of the top in the nation!

Graduate and Get Hired

It takes a lot of resources to work your way through college and get a degree. However, the University of Bulldogs doesn't stop at just teaching you what you need to know. We work directly with industry leaders to get real world experience for our students while they are going to school and help place you in the right job after graduation.
